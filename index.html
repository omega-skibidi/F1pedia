WHAT IS FORMULA ONE ?

➡️Formula 1 is the top-tier, open wheel,single seater motorsport around the world. With cars touching upto 370 Km/h.

➡️There are 11 teams with 2 drivers per team racing across 24+ GRAND PRIX in one season across the world managed by the FIA

What is a Grand Prix ?

➡️A grand prix(french for grand prize)is a weekend-long racing event which is one round of 1 formula one season

➡️ one circuit track spans across 280-300 kms divided into the start-finish straits, different types of turns and bends and straits

For more GRAND PRX information access the navbar👆

The things which tell drivers whats ahead - FLAGS🎌

➡️There are 10 official flags in formula one. They are:

  1. 🟥Red
  2. 🟨Yellow
  3. 🟩Green
  4. ⬜White
  5. ⬛Black
  6. 🟦Blue
  7. ⬛⬜Black & White
  8. 🟨🟥Yellow & Red striped
  9. ⬛🟧Black & Orange
  10. 🏁Chequred

What do all mean ? 🤷‍♂️

🟥Red flag

It means that all the cars have to stop and return to the pits

this is usually due to bad weather, a big crash or other unsafe conditions

🟨Yellow flag

this usually means that there is debis on the track so cars have to slow down and no overtaking

double yellow flag means that big debris on track also may lead to a safety car

🟩Green flag

This means the racing session has begun or resumed

Full racing is allowed and the track is clear

⬜White flag

This usually means there is a slower car(mostly damaged) ahead

So this is precautionary

⬛Black flag

This means trouble for the driver

It means the driver is disqualified form the race/session
